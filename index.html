About Me

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Edinburgh and a researcher at the Generative AI Laboratory (GAIL). With a formal background in geometry and first-author publications at top-tier conferences, my expertise spans differentiable rendering, 3D reconstruction, and geometric deep learning. My current research focuses on equipping world models with temporally stable 3D scene representations.
