About Me

Hello! I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the University of Edinburgh and a researcher at the Generative AI Laboratory (GAIL). My research spans differentiable rendering, 3D reconstruction, and 3D generative models. Before starting my PhD I studied Mathematics and Physics at the University of Durham, with a focus on the geometrical aspects of gauge field theories. Following this, I became fascinated by geometric deep learning, and completed an MSci in Artifical Intelligence at the University Of Edinburgh. Since then I've largely worked towards developing 3D reconstruction, from images and medical scanners such as CT/MRI. My current research focuses on equipping generative world models with temporally stable 3D scene representations.

I am currently looking for both collaborators and internships, so feel free to reach out!
